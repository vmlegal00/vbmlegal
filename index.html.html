Biography

Trusted Legal Counsel in Mumbai & Thane

Adv. Vikas B. Mourya is a practising advocate based in Bandra West, Mumbai, with a robust practice spanning the Supreme Court of India, the Bombay High Court, Sessions Courts and MM Courts across Mumbai and Thane.

He holds a B.Com, LL.B, and an LL.M in Constitutional Law — a combination that gives him a sharp analytical edge in constitutional challenges, fundamental rights matters, and complex civil litigation, while his commerce background adds depth to financial and property disputes.

Whether you are in Bandra, Khar, Santacruz, Andheri, or anywhere across Mumbai and Thane, Adv. Mourya provides dedicated, client-first legal representation — from the first consultation through to the final judgment.

His practice spans Criminal Law, Civil Litigation, Family Law, Constitutional Law, Property Law, and legal documentation — making VBM Legal a comprehensive one-stop legal practice for individuals and businesses alike.
